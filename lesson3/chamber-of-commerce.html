Valley Floor Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a design planning document for a larger website about the Valley Floor Chamber of Commerce. It provides an overview of the organization, including its demographics as well as information on the design elements such as color scheme and font choices. The site is divided into three pages, each with a specific focus. The first page provides general information about the Valley Floor Chamber of Commerce, the second page is dedicated to persona cards and demographic information, and the last page provides detailed design information, including the color scheme and fonts. This site serves as a guide for the design and development of the larger website, and it is intended to be used by the website development team to ensure a cohesive and effective design.
