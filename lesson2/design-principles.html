Lesson 2: Design Principles

Repetition

Steam

The sales page does a wonderful job with repetition by having a uniform look across the discounts and sales prices of each game. All the games in this section are the same size, the percentages have the same color and typeface, and the discounts all share a similar feel of showing an original price and a discounted price.

Rule of Thirds

Minecraft

The home page of Minecraft follows the rule of thirds very well by having the main text, "WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL SITE OF MINECRAFT", aligned with the top third line, where the user's eyes are most likely to go first. Then on the bottom third line, there's a button to purchase and download the game.

Contrast

Minecraft

The home page of Chess.com, LLC has a contrasting color scheme that makes it easy to see where things are and what's important. They have a dark colored background with white text, and an accent colorto highlight the log in button and the button to start a chess game.
